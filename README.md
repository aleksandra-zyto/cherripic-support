 # CherriPic Support

  Welcome! CherriPic helps you organize your photos into beautiful albums with simple swipes.

  ## How to Use CherriPic

  1. **Sign in** with your Apple ID
  2. **Select a date range** - pick a month or custom dates
  3. **Swipe through photos** - right to keep, left to skip
  4. **Create your album** - all kept photos go into a new album
  5. **Track your progress** - see stats and all your created albums

  ## Features

  - Create curated albums from any date range
  - Delete photos while organizing (long-press)
  - Custom album names
  - Track all your albums and stats
  - Dark mode interface
  - Completely free - no subscriptions

  **Common Questions:**

  **Q: Why does the app need photo access?**
  A: CherriPic needs access to read your photos and create albums. All processing happens on your device -
   photos never leave your phone.

  **Q: Are my photos uploaded anywhere?**
  A: No! All organization happens locally on your device. Your photos stay private.

  **Q: Can I undo deleting photos?**
  A: Deleted photos go to your Recently Deleted album for 30 days before permanent deletion.

  **Q: How do I delete my account?**
  A: Sign out in the app Settings. Your data will be automatically removed after 30 days.
