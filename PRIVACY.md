  # Privacy Policy for CherriPic

  **Last updated: December 1, 2025**

  ## Overview

  CherriPic ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what
   data we collect and how we use it.

  ## Information We Collect

  ### Authentication Data
  We use **Sign in with Apple** for secure authentication. We collect:
  - **Apple User ID** (unique identifier, required)
  - **Email address** (optional - only if you choose to share)
  - **Name** (optional - only if you choose to share)

  ### Usage Statistics
  To provide the Progress feature, we store:
  - Number of photos organized
  - Number of albums created
  - Date ranges you've selected
  - Keep/skip/delete counts per session

  ### Your Photos
  **IMPORTANT:** Your photos are NEVER uploaded to our servers.
  - All photo processing happens **locally on your device**
  - Photos remain in your device's photo library
  - We never see, access, or store your actual photos
  - Album creation happens entirely on your device

  ## How We Use Your Information

  We use collected data to:
  - Authenticate your account (Apple User ID)
  - Display your organization statistics in the Progress tab
  - Improve app functionality
  - Provide customer support (if requested)

  ## Data Storage

  - **Authentication data**: Stored securely on our backend servers
  - **Usage statistics**: Stored to provide the Progress feature
  - **Your photos**: NEVER stored on our servers - stay on your device only

  ## Third-Party Services

  We use the following services:

  ### Sign in with Apple
  - Provided by Apple Inc.
  - Handles all authentication
  - [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)

  ### Backend Hosting
  - Hosted on Render.com
  - Stores only user authentication and statistics (no photos)
  - [Render Privacy Policy](https://render.com/privacy)

  ## Your Photos & Privacy

  - ✅ All photo organization happens **on your device**
  - ✅ Photos **never leave your device**
  - ✅ We cannot see your photos
  - ✅ We cannot access your photo library
  - ✅ Album creation is local only

  ## Your Rights

  You have the right to:
  - **Access your data**: View what data we store
  - **Delete your data**: Remove all your information
  - **Revoke app access**: Settings → Apple ID → Apps Using Apple ID → CherriPic

  ## Data Deletion

  To delete your account and all associated data:

  1. **Log out** in the app (Settings → Log Out)
  2. **Revoke access** in iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Apps Using Apple ID → CherriPic → Stop Using Apple
  ID

  Your data will be automatically removed from our servers within 30 days.

  Alternatively, [create a support issue](https://github.com/aleksandra-zyto/cherripic-support/issues/new)
  requesting account deletion.

  ## Children's Privacy

  CherriPic is rated 4+ and suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from
  children under 13.

  ## Photo Library Permission

  CherriPic requires **photo library read/write access** to:
  - Display photos for organization
  - Create albums with selected photos
  - Delete photos (when requested by you)

  **This permission is used ONLY locally on your device.** Photos are never uploaded.

  ## Changes to This Policy

  We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through
  the app or via email (if provided).

  Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

  ## Data Security

  We implement industry-standard security measures:
  - Secure HTTPS connections
  - Encrypted data storage
  - Sign in with Apple authentication
  - No password storage (handled by Apple)
